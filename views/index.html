Welcome to My Tutoring Website for CS61A

The Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs

What This Is

Whether you're prepping for a midterm, shadowing the course, or even just interested in computer science in general, this website represents the resources I found useful to learn the material in CS61A.

What This Has

Click through the tabs on the top to access resources I have created or gathered that will help prepare you for CS61A!

Excluding the worksheets I've made, a lot of these resources can be found via the CS61A website, but they may be organized in a way better suited for you!

What You Get

CS61A is an extraordinary class and a great intro to programming. However, when I was taking the course, I noticed that a lot of my peers weren't aware of all the available resources on the material outside of the classroom.

To master the material is to practice as much as you can, there aren't any shortcuts to it! The more you put into your introductory CS course, I think the better foundation you set for future courses in the subject.
